Pricing FAQ

Are upgrades pro-rated?

Yes. When you change plans mid-cycle, we only charge the difference for the remainder of the period.

Any hidden fees?

No hidden fees. Taxes may apply depending on your region.

Can I switch regions?

Yes — take a snapshot and restore in the new region for minimal downtime.
